Frassati Society

Purpose/Mission:

The Columbus Frassati Society follows in the footsteps of Blessed Pier Giorgio Frassati, seeking to bring the whole person to Christ through adoration, study, community, charitable activity, and love of the outdoors. Young adults ages 21-35 in the Columbus area are welcome to join.

Activities:

Christ in the City - holy hour and socializing at 7pm on the fourth Thursday each month

Brunch after Mass monthly

Rotating variety of events year-round: ice-skating, concerts, sporting events, game nights

History:
Audience:

Young adults, single or married, ages 21-35
